## What is Copilot Vision?
GitHub Copilot Vision extends AI-assisted coding by allowing developers to attach images and PDFs directly within chat prompts. This enables the AI to interpret visual and document-based context alongside your code.

## Why Does This Matter?
By understanding screenshots, diagrams, or documentation files, Copilot can provide smarter suggestions and assist with debugging or understanding complex contexts that were previously text-only.

## Enhancing Developer Productivity
This multi-modal approach reduces friction where contextual visuals are crucial, fostering clearer communication and more efficient problem-solving within the coding workflow.

As Copilot Vision becomes generally available, it offers developers greater synergy between visual assets and AI-enhanced coding assistance, shaping the future of collaborative programming.
