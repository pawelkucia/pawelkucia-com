## Introduction to GitHub Copilot for Jira

GitHub Copilot for Jira is now generally available, bringing AI-powered assistance to streamline your project management experience. This tool integrates the power of GitHub Copilot directly into Jira.

## Evolution Through Feedback

Since the public preview launched in March 2026, several enhancements have been introduced. Notably, the addition of model selection allows users to tailor AI assistance to their specific needs.

## Impact on Project Management

By embedding AI capabilities into Jira, teams can expect improved productivity and smarter workflows. This release signals a step forward in making project management more efficient and adaptive.
