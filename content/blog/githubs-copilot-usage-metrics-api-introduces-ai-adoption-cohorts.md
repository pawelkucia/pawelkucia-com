## Moving Beyond Activity: Understanding Copilot Usage

GitHub's Copilot usage metrics API has received a significant update: it now categorizes engaged users into AI adoption cohorts. This means it's no longer just about who is actively using Copilot but also how different users incorporate the AI tool into their development processes.

## What Are AI Adoption Cohorts?

These cohorts classify users based on their interaction patterns with Copilot, offering richer insights into adoption levels. It highlights varied user behaviors, from early adopters experimenting with Copilot to those who have fully integrated it into their workflows.

## Why It Matters

By capturing deeper usage patterns, organizations and teams can better understand the real-world impact of AI assistance. This informs decisions on training, implementation strategies, and continuous improvement of AI tools aligned with developer needs.

GitHub’s initiative marks an important step in enhancing transparency and usability of AI metrics in software development environments.
