## Enhanced Flexibility for Copilot Business and Enterprise Teams

GitHub has introduced a new feature in Visual Studio Code for Copilot Business and Enterprise users: Bring Your Own Key (BYOK). This capability allows organizations to bring and reuse their own API keys to access language models.

## What Does BYOK Bring to Your Workflow?

BYOK empowers teams to maintain tighter control over their AI model usage by leveraging their own API keys. This improves security, compliance, and billing management, creating a more seamless integration experience.

## Why This Matters

As AI becomes integral to developer workflows, having control over the underlying models and keys is important for enterprises that prioritize data governance and customization. This move by GitHub reflects the growing demand for flexible and secure AI tooling.

## Final Thoughts

The addition of BYOK in VS Code for Copilot Business and Enterprise users marks a significant step toward empowering organizations with more control and adaptability in integrating AI-driven tools into their software development lifecycle.
