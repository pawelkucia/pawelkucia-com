## Expanding Secret Scanning Capabilities

GitHub continues to improve its platform security by updating its secret scanning features. One key enhancement is the detection of new secret types, including those from APIclub, which broadens the scope of automatically identified sensitive information.

## Partnership with Resend

In a move to further boost secret scanning, Resend has become an official GitHub secret scanning partner. This partnership indicates GitHub's commitment to collaborate closely with services to enhance detection accuracy and proactive security measures.

## Strengthening Public Monitoring

Alongside secret scanning improvements, public monitoring capabilities have also been refined. These enhancements help developers and organizations to monitor exposed secrets more effectively and respond quicker to potential security incidents.

## Conclusion

Such developments in GitHub's security tooling empower developers to safeguard their projects more efficiently, reducing the risk of accidental credential exposure in code repositories.
