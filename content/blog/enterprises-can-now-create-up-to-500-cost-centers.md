## Expanded Capacity for Cost Centers
GitHub has increased the maximum number of cost centers in an enterprise from 250 to 500. This change accommodates larger enterprises with complex organizational structures, such as hundreds or even thousands of business units, departments, or product lines.

## Benefits for Large Organizations
Managing financial data for diverse segments is critical for transparency and accountability. By doubling cost center limits, organizations gain enhanced ability to track expenses across multiple divisions, enabling more accurate budgeting and cost control.

## Implications for Financial Oversight
This improvement simplifies financial reporting and allocation for enterprises, ensuring each segment can be analyzed in greater detail. The update supports operational efficiency and improves strategic decision-making.

Overall, this update by GitHub facilitates better financial management for enterprises with extensive, multifaceted structures.
