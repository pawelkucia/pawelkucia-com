## Enforcing License Compliance Across Dependencies

Enterprises increasingly rely on open source software, making license compliance a critical concern. GitHub’s new open source license compliance feature, now in public preview, offers a sophisticated way to manage compliance at scale.

## Ruleset-Based Checks For Centralized Policy

The feature introduces ruleset-based checks that allow organizations to define and enforce centralized policies for open source licenses. This means dependencies can be automatically blocked if they do not comply with company license requirements.

## Streamlining Dependency Governance

By integrating license checks directly into the development workflow, enterprises can mitigate risks and maintain clarity over their software supply chain. This advancement helps maintain governance without slowing down innovation.

Embracing such tools is a step toward responsible and scalable software development in an era dominated by open source technologies.
