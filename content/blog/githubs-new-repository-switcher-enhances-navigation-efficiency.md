## Streamlined Repository Navigation

Managing multiple repositories just became more efficient with GitHub's new repository switcher. Available now in the global navigation, this feature lets developers jump between repositories without leaving their current page.

## How It Works

Simply select the chevron next to the repository name to reveal the switcher. This smooth transition means no more loading delays or extra clicks, which helps maintain focus and saves time.

## Why It Matters

Developers often juggle various projects simultaneously. Reducing friction in navigation supports a smoother workflow and increases productivity across teams and individuals alike.
