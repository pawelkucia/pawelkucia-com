## Granular Control over Copilot Models for Enterprises

GitHub has rolled out a powerful feature that lets enterprise owners control exactly which Copilot models are accessible within each organization. This means you can tailor AI assistance to fit specific compliance standards or team preferences.

## What Are Targeted Model Rules?

Targeted model rules enable organizations to assign different GitHub Copilot models to different teams or units. Instead of a one-size-fits-all approach, enterprises now gain the flexibility to optimize AI usage according to distinct needs.

## Why This Matters

This granular model management supports regulatory compliance, security policies, and specialized workflows. It also enhances efficiency by aligning AI capabilities with project or team requirements.

Enterprises looking to deepen their AI integration can now do so with greater confidence and alignment to their internal practices.
