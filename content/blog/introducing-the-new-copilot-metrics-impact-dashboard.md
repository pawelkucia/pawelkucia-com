## A Deeper Look into Copilot Usage

GitHub has released a new Copilot metrics impact dashboard aimed at enterprise administrators and organization owners. This dashboard provides detailed insights into how Copilot is being utilized across your teams.

## Moving Beyond Basic Usage

Instead of just knowing who is using Copilot, the dashboard allows admins to tell a more comprehensive impact story. This helps to quantify value and showcase productivity gains with precision.

## Enhancing Decision-Making

With richer data, organizations can optimize their AI-assisted workflows and make informed decisions that support innovation and efficiency. This tool marks a significant step toward smarter AI adoption in enterprise development environments.
