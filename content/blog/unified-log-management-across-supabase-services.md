## Centralized Log Access for Supabase Services

Debugging and monitoring applications often involve juggling logs from multiple sources. Supabase introduces a unified view that consolidates logs across all its services into a single searchable interface.

## Live Tail and Filtering

Stay updated with real-time log tails that allow developers to see events as they happen. Enhanced filtering options make it easier to pinpoint relevant logs faster, reducing overhead in troubleshooting.

## Timeline Visualization

A timeline feature provides a chronological view of log events, helping in correlating activities and understanding system behavior over time.

This integrated approach improves operational efficiency and lets developers focus more on development and less on log management.
