## New Desktop Experience for Developers

GitHub has released the Copilot app in technical preview, providing a native desktop environment tailored for AI-assisted development. This app brings the power of agentic development to your desk, allowing you to interact more intuitively with the AI tools directly from your workflow.

## Focus and Control in Coding

The Copilot app enables developers to keep their work isolated and steer the AI interactions effectively, helping maintain focus without disruptions. This integration can enhance productivity while ensuring that the development process stays clear and manageable.

## The Future of AI-Powered Development

With the Copilot app, GitHub is pushing forward the way developers engage with AI, fostering more natural collaboration between human and machine. This technical preview invites feedback to refine these interactions and shape the future of coding assistance.
