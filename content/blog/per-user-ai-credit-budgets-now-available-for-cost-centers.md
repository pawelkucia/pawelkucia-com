## Enhanced Control with Per-user AI Credit Budgets

Enterprise administrators now have the ability to assign a single AI credit budget per user within each cost center. This budget applies uniformly to every individual associated with that cost center, allowing for consistent and granular financial management.

## Dynamic Adaptation to Team Changes

As team members join or leave, the budgets automatically adjust, ensuring that your cost center budgets accurately reflect current usage without manual intervention.

## Benefits for Enterprises

- Improved visibility over AI consumption
- Easier budget forecasting
- More precise allocation of resources

This update simplifies tracking and controlling AI expenditures across teams, enabling enterprises to balance innovation with cost efficiency.
