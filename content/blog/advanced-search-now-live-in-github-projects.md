## Enhanced Filtering with Advanced Search

The GitHub Projects filter bar has been upgraded with advanced search capabilities. Users can now combine logical AND and OR operators to build detailed and precise views.

## Why This Matters

Previously, managing complex filters often required maintaining separate views or toggling multiple filters. With advanced search, organizing tasks, issues, or pull requests becomes more efficient and less error-prone.

## Benefits for Teams

This feature strengthens project visibility and control, enabling teams to tailor dashboards exactly to their workflow and priorities. It’s a meaningful step toward simplifying project management at scale.

Harness these capabilities to streamline your GitHub workflow with more clarity and precision.
